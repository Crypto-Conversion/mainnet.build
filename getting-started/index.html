Noxon

Co-Founder & CEO of swap.online

Our goal is to create an ecosystem for decentralized exchange of various cryptocurrencies, tokens and assets. For such purpose we use Atomic Swap technology, which allows us to perform cross blockchain transactions, unlike classic DEXes. To store orders we are using our own unique technology of decentralized order book based on IPFS. We have already completed two standalone products - hot wallet and the fastest exchange - you can already test it to exchange ETH, BTC and ERC-20 tokens - it will take less than 5 minutes. We also doing a lot of work on UI design, to simplify it as much as we can - to show people, that it is not that difficult to interact with crypto. Moreover, our project is absolutely open sourced - just check our github!

And now we have discovered, that stack of our developments could be used to create a really amazing and innovative product - the first platform for decentralized IEO - where all participants have the equal rights to buy tokens and set an order to sell at the same order. Transparency is granted by blockchain itself, all processing is being controlled by smart contracts - neither project, nor we could affect anyhow on IEO.
